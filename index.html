How it works

A simple flow: a short prep with your champion, then we bring the session to your office. No heavy lift — we meet you where you are.

1

Prep session with your champion

30–45 minutes · Remote or in-person

  • Meet with a customer champion and a Glean Solutions Engineer.
  • Align on your current tools, workflows, AI initiatives, and success metrics.
  • Decide which teams, processes, and use cases to prioritize in the on-site session.
2

On-site AI session at your office

60–90 minutes · Breakfast, lunch, or coffee

  • Host a broader group from IT, engineering, operations, HR, and business stakeholders.
  • Explore the AI landscape at work today and how teams are using Work AI.
  • Walk through customer-proven workflows and agents running in production.
  • Discuss your environment, data sources, and constraints — in plain language.
3

Follow-up and next steps

Optional recap and planning

  • Receive an optional written recap or action list from the session.
  • Get suggestions for pilot ideas, phased rollout, or deeper evaluation.
  • Align on where Work AI can drive the most impact for your teams.

Frequently asked questions

What formats do you offer?

Breakfast, lunch-and-learn, or coffee/afternoon sessions. We'll align on the format that fits your schedule.

Where do the sessions take place?

At your office in the selected region. We confirm the exact location with your champion during the prep call.

Is there a cost to host a session?

The session is complimentary for invited organizations. Glean covers lunch/refreshments for the Lunch & Learn format.

How many people should attend?

We recommend 6–20 attendees—a mix of technical and business stakeholders. Smaller groups are welcome.

No. The prep call covers your environment; the on-site session focuses on discussion, examples, and planning with product views where relevant.

Will you cover our specific environment and questions?

Yes. The prep call aligns content to your tools, data sources, and teams.
