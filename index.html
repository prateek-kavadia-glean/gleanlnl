About the sessions

A limited number of on-site AI strategy sessions: our team comes to your office to help you put Work AI to work. No generic pitch — we tailor the conversation to your tools, your teams, and your goals.

Each session is designed to meet you where you are in your AI journey—whether you’re just starting to explore AI at work or refining an existing roadmap. The format is flexible: breakfast briefing, lunch-and-learn, coffee chat, or afternoon working session.

  • We join you in your office for a focused 60–90 minute session.
  • We tailor content based on your current tools, processes, and priorities.
  • We use real customer examples and live product walkthroughs to ground the discussion.
  • You leave with clearer next steps for your AI plans and pilots.

How it works

A simple flow: a short prep with your champion, then we bring the session to your office. No heavy lift — we meet you where you are.

1

30–45 minutes · Remote or in-person

  • Meet with a customer champion and a Glean Solutions Engineer.
  • Align on your current tools, workflows, AI initiatives, and success metrics.
  • Decide which teams, processes, and use cases to prioritize in the on-site session.
2

On-site AI session at your office

60–90 minutes · Breakfast, lunch, or coffee

  • Host a broader group from IT, engineering, operations, HR, and business stakeholders.
  • Explore the AI landscape at work today and how teams are using Work AI.
  • Walk through customer-proven workflows and agents running in production.
  • Discuss your environment, data sources, and constraints — in plain language.
3

Follow-up and next steps

Optional recap and planning

  • Receive an optional written recap or action list from the session.
  • Get suggestions for pilot ideas, phased rollout, or deeper evaluation.
  • Align on where Work AI can drive the most impact for your teams.

Frequently asked questions

What formats do you offer?

We offer breakfast briefings, lunch-and-learns, coffee chats, and afternoon working sessions. We’ll work with you to choose the format that best fits your culture and schedule.

Where do the sessions take place?

All sessions take place at your office in the selected city or region. We’ll confirm the exact location with your champion during the prep conversation.

Is there a cost to host a session?

The on-site AI strategy session itself is complimentary for invited organizations. If catering or additional services are involved, we’ll align on details in advance.

How many people should attend?

We typically recommend 6–20 attendees, including a mix of technical and business stakeholders. Smaller working groups are welcome as well.

Do we need any specific tools set up in advance?

No special setup is required. The prep session will cover your existing tools and environment. The on-site session focuses on discussion, examples, and planning, with product views where relevant.

Will you cover our specific environment and questions?

Yes. The goal of the prep session is to understand your tools, data sources, and priorities so that the on-site conversation speaks directly to your context and teams.

How soon will we hear back after submitting a request?

You can expect to hear from us within business days to coordinate your prep session and confirm next steps.
